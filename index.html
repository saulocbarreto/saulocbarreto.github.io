Intro

I'm Saulo, I'm 26 years old, and I'm passionate about nature, technology, and music. Right now I live in Debrecen, Hungary but I was born in the northeast of Brazil.

Two things drive me towards my professional goals: i) the chance to work with something new, diving into the unknown, and ii) the fulfillment of seeing a completed project that started as a non-reproducible event in someone's mind.

Generally speaking, during my Bachelor I realized that the Computer Science field would definitely be my first choice when deciding my career. And from that point, I've been developing my programming and soft skills in a timeline far from a predictable one but exciting as it could be.

As the years went by, I noticed that in all my projects I adopted the learning as you go strategy. And I can confidently say that the most important thing in this process was simply accepting the challenge.

Work & Education

At this point, I'll provide you a glimpse of what I've done so far being succinct as possible. Details about the projects can be found on my CV.

  • 2012

  • March, 2012

    BSc. in Mechatronics Engineering

    I started my studies at Universidade de Brasília just after I left high school. At that point, I only knew that I wanted to pursue a path in something related to technology and innovation, and a curriculum including Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer Science subjects was more than attractive.

  • April, 2012

    Young Talents for Science

    While I was completing the Calculus and Physics disciplines, I could participate in this program funded by CAPES, and that was the first time that I came across the scientific method, hypothesis tests, and literature review.

  • 2014

  • August, 2014

    Science without Borders Program

    This is one of the most life-changing experiences in this timeline. After being selected to study at Óbuda University in Budapest for one year, I realized how much impact living abroad can have. The chance to experience a completely different educational method, and the fact that you're forced to increase your comfort zone once you realize you're on your own definitely had a great influence in the person I became.

  • October, 2014

    Antal Bejczy Center for Intelligent Robotics

    Trying to get the most out of the exchange experience, I worked at this lab, and at some point, I realized that the effort that I was putting into my projects didn't feel like effort. The satisfaction of going to the lab to do something that I enjoyed left a remarkable feeling in my mind.

  • 2017

  • January, 2017

    Office of Federal Prosecution

    Before working as an intern for prosecutors and lawyers I never had a true knowledge of the importance of documentation and word choice while working with technical documents. Moreover, the Helpdesk part of that job made me realize how the user experience is intrinsically connected to the ability to put yourself in someone else's shoes.

  • July, 2017

    Final BSc. Work

    During my final project I could start learning about the Machine Learning field and it was a great opportunity to put together all the knowledge that I had so far.

  • 2019

  • January, 2019

    Digital Marketing Freelancer

    This is the point where things might seem to deviate from an expected path. After being invited by a friend I started to work and learn how to edit videos for online courses in different fields, to create landing pages, ads. Lastly, I also learned about human behavior, adjusting client's expectations, and how important it is to comprehend their needs.

  • December, 2019

    IoT Developer

    As a freelancer, I was hired to provide a prototype for a system that would measure the water flow using a Raspberry Pi to collect, send the data, and interpret it.

  • 2020

  • September, 2020

    MSc. in Mechatronics Engineering

    After getting a scholarship and facing all the uncertainties of the pandemic, I started my masters at Debrecen, the second-largest city in Hungary, but definitely the charmest. In the academic environment, I'm pursuing a specialization in Cyber-Physical systems working in AI and Computer vision-related projects.

  • 2021

  • May, 2021

    Developer

    Currently, I'm learning Vue.js and Socket.io frameworks in order to build an interface for sensor monitoring. The project is funded by the Ministry for Innovation and Technology under the association of the MIT Venture Mentoring Service and is supported by the University of Debrecen.
