README

Considering the limitations of a CV and in order to introduce me, this website will give you a perspective considering both a personal and a professional point of view.

I'm Saulo, I'm 26 years old, and I'm passionate about nature, technology and music. Right now I live in Debrecen, Hungary but I was born in the northeast of Brazil. Generally speaking, during my Bachelor I realized that the Computer Science field would definitely be my first choice when deciding my carrer. The fact that I could choose one of the three big fields from Mechatronics (Electrical, Mechanical and Computer Science) gave me the freedom and knowledge to make a conscious choice.

One of the things that makes me fulfilled is to take a closer look at a finished project that worked alone or in a team and to reflect about how an idea starts as a blank space and ends up as something that will have real impact and usefulness. Before I start rambling about life let's move on to the next section.
